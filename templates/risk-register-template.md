# Risk Register Template

This template provides a structured format for identifying, tracking, and mitigating risks during program execution.

Risk registers help program leadership maintain visibility into uncertainties that may affect delivery and ensure ownership and mitigation actions are clearly defined.

## Field Guidance

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Risk ID | Unique identifier for the risk |
| Date Identified | Date the risk was first documented |
| Target Resolution Date | Expected date the risk will be mitigated or reduced to acceptable levels |
| Last Reviewed | Most recent date the risk was reviewed |
| Risk Description | Clear description of the potential issue |
| Impact | Potential effect on program delivery or outcomes |
| Likelihood | Estimated probability of the risk occurring |
| Risk Owner | Individual responsible for monitoring and mitigating the risk |
| Mitigation Strategy | Planned actions to reduce the likelihood or impact |
| Status | Monitoring, Active Mitigation, Resolved, or Escalated |
| Escalation | Indicates whether the risk requires escalation to program governance |

---

## Usage

Risk registers are typically reviewed during regular program cadence meetings to ensure risks remain visible and mitigation actions are progressing.

Risks that cannot be mitigated within the delivery team or program leadership authority should be escalated through the governance structure described in:

`docs/02-governance-model.md`
